Masterclass Moving

A friend of mine had started a moving company and I offered to make him a website, as it would be good practice for me and hopefully help him to get more business. I used free images along with images and content found on his facebook page and filled in a majority of the rest editing answers from chatGPT. I put a lot of research into other moving companies, from the content of their websites, to colors and images, and even their design and layouts.

  • Edited ChatGPT answers and owners facebook page descriptions for content
  • Researched images and other companies to get ideas
